Using the Website

The Experience of Landscape Workers website offers some core features:

  • To access the main functionality of the website you'll need to log into your account
  • If you don't yet have an account, you'll need to create one
  • Once logged in you'll be able to share an experience by adding a new entry to your journal
  • You can also read, update, and delete your existing journal entries
  • If you're offline or want to make a rough draft of a journal entry to finish later, you can write drafts
