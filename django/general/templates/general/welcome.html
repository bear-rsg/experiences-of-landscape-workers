Experiences of Landscape Workers

Welcome

Welcome to the Experiences of Landscape Workers website.

Experiences of Landscape Workers is a research project led by Jeremy Kidwell at the University of Birmingham in the UK.

It facilitates ethnographic study into the spiritual and social experience of landscape workers (biodynamic farmers, energetic herbalists, etc.) around the UK.

Using the Website

The Experience of Landscape Workers website offers some core features:

  • To access the main functionality of the website you'll need to log into your account
  • If you don't yet have an account, you'll need to create one
  • Once logged in you'll be able to share an experience by adding a new entry to your journal
  • You can also read, update, and delete your existing journal entries
  • If you're offline or want to make a rough draft of a journal entry to finish later, you can write drafts

Support

If you require support to access and use this website, or if you'd like to report any issues, please email j.kidwell@bham.ac.uk
